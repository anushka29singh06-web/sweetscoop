Our Story

Born from a Passion for Perfection

Sweet Scoops was born in 2020 from a single dream — to create ice cream that wasn't just a dessert, but a moment of pure joy. Our founder, inspired by family recipes and global flavours, set out to craft something extraordinary for the people of Bhopal.

Every flavour we create begins with the finest ingredients — hand-selected mangoes from Alphonso orchards, single-origin Belgian chocolate, farm-fresh cream, and spices sourced directly from Kerala. We never compromise.
